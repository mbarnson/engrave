## Decisions Made
- LilyPond `es` treated as E-flat (abbreviation of `ees`), `as` as A-flat (abbreviation of `aes`) -- standard LilyPond behavior, not the naive `e` + `s` (E-sharp) interpretation
- Regex accidental patterns ordered longest-first (`ees` before `es`, `aes` before `as`) to prevent partial match ambiguity
- Dynamic markings attached to `note.expressions` list as `music21.dynamics.Dynamic` objects -- simplest attachment point for standalone notes before stream insertion
- music21 9.9.1 added as project dependency (was planned for Phase 9 evaluation but needed now for MusicXML export)
